About Me

I'm Katelen Valdez, a passionate web design student dedicated to creating engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. My journey into web design began with a curiosity for technology and a desire to blend creativity with functionality.

With a keen eye for design and a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I strive to craft websites and applications that not only look beautiful but also deliver seamless functionality and optimal user experience.

Driven by a constant desire to learn and grow, I am always exploring new technologies and design trends to stay ahead in the ever-evolving field of web development. Whether it's building responsive layouts, optimizing performance, or experimenting with new frameworks, I'm always up for a challenge.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring nature, working out at the gym, or trying new coffee shops. I believe that inspiration can be found everywhere, and I draw from diverse experiences to fuel my creativity.
